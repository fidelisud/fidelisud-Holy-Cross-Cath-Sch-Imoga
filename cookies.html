Cookies Policy

At Holy Cross Catholic School, we are committed to protecting your privacy in line with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA). This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them on our website, and your choices regarding them. By continuing to use our Site, you consent to our use of cookies as described below (except where explicit consent is required)

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit, such as preferences or login details, to improve your experience

We use only essential cookies that are strictly necessary for the Site to function properly. We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies that collect personal data for marketing or profiling purposes

2. Cookies We Use

Our website uses the following categories of cookies:

Essential / Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for basic website functionality (e.g., navigation, security, and loading pages correctly). They do not require consent under NDPA rules.

  • Purpose: Enable core Site features like secure page loading and basic navigation.
  • Duration: Session-based (deleted when you close your browser) or short-term persistent.
  • No personal data is collected beyond what is needed for functionality

Other Cookies (Not Currently Used)

We do not currently use:

  • Performance / Analytics Cookies (e.g., to count visitors or see popular pages)
  • Functional Cookies (e.g., remembering language or form preferences)
  • Targeting / Advertising Cookies

If we ever add such cookies in the future, we will update this policy and obtain your explicit consent via a clear cookie banner (with "Accept" / "Reject" options) before deployment

3. Consent and Control

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 and NDPC guidelines:

  • Essential cookies do not require consent.
  • Any non-essential cookies would require your freely given, informed and specific consent (no pre-ticked boxes)

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies (may affect Site functionality)
  • Delete cookies after each visit
  • Receive alerts when cookies are set

Most browsers allow cookie management via Settings → Privacy/Security. For help:

  • Chrome: chrome://settings/content/cookies
  • Firefox: about:preferences#privacy
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy

4. Third-Party Cookies

We do not embed third-party content (e.g., social media widgets, YouTube videos, or analytics tools) that would place additional cookies. If we add any in the future, we will disclose them here and seek consent where required

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law or our practices. The updated version will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance
