Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our school, admissions and community

What grades do we offer at Holy Cross Catholic School Imoga?

We currently provide Education from Nursery to Secondary Education level. We focus on foundational Catholic values, strong academics and character formation for young learners

Do students need to be Catholic to attend?

No. While we are proudly Catholic and integrate faith into daily life (including prayers, Mass and religious instruction), we warmly welcome students and families of all faiths and backgrounds who respect our mission and values

What are the school hours?

School operates Monday to Friday:
7:30 AM – 2:00 PM
After-school care and extracurricular activities may extend hours — please inquire

How do I apply for admission?

Admission is open year-round subject to availability. Steps include:
1. Obtain and complete an application form from the school office
2. Submit birth certificate, previous school reports and passport photos
3. Attend an entrance assessment/interview (for new students).
4. Pay registration fees upon acceptance.
Contact the office for current deadlines and requirements

What is the uniform policy?

Students wear a neat, modest uniform that reflects our Catholic identity. Uniform items can be purchased in school

Do you offer transportation or school bus service?

At present we do not operate school buses. Many families arrange private transport or carpooling. We are happy to help connect families for shared rides where possible

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer catechism, choir, altar serving, football, athletics, debate club, arts & crafts and seasonal activities. These help develop talents and build community while reinforcing Christian values

How is discipline handled?

Discipline is rooted in love, respect and restorative justice in line with Catholic teaching. We emphasize positive reinforcement, counseling and parental partnership. Serious matters follow the school's behavior policy
