Privacy Policy

At Holy Cross Catholic School Imoga, we value the trust placed in us by our students, parents, staff and visitors This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect and share personal information in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) and our Catholic mission of respect for human dignity

1. Who We Are

Holy Cross Catholic School Imoga ("we", "us", "our") is a private educational institution located in Imogo Edo State, Nigeria

We act as a data controller for the personal data we collect directly (e.g., through this website, enrollment forms, or school events)

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Contact & Identification: names, email addresses, phone numbers, home addresses (parents/guardians, students, staff)
  • Student & Educational: date of birth, previous school records, health information (where relevant for welfare), photographs/videos (with consent)
  • Website Usage: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time/date of access (via cookies/analytics – minimal & anonymized where possible)
  • Other: information voluntarily provided in inquiries, forms or communications

3. How We Collect Information

We collect data:

  • Directly from you (enrollment forms, inquiries, emails, phone calls)
  • From third parties (previous schools – with consent where required)
  • Automatically via this website (limited cookies & server logs)
  • During school activities (photos/videos of events – only with explicit consent)

4. How We Use Your Information

We process personal data for legitimate purposes, including:

  • Providing education and pastoral care
  • Admissions, enrollment and administrative functions
  • Communicating school updates, newsletters, and emergency notices
  • Complying with legal obligations (e.g., child safeguarding, NDPA)
  • Maintaining school security and safety
  • With consent: publishing photos/videos on our website/social media or in promotional materials

5. Lawful Basis for Processing

Our processing is based on:

  • Legal obligation (e.g., education laws, child protection)
  • Contract (enrollment agreement with parents/guardians)
  • Legitimate interests (school administration, safety – balanced against your rights)
  • Consent (for photos, marketing emails, optional activities – freely given & easy to withdraw)

6. Special Note on Children’s Data

We take extra care with students under 18 (considered children under Nigerian law). Parental/guardian consent is required for:

  • Processing non-essential data (e.g., photos for website/social media)
  • Any online services requiring consent

We do not knowingly collect data from children without appropriate parental involvement

7. Sharing Your Information

We share data only when necessary:

  • With school staff & authorized personnel
  • With regulators, government bodies or child protection authorities (when legally required)
  • With service providers (e.g., cloud storage, email provider) under strict data protection agreements
  • We do NOT sell personal data to third parties

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss or misuse, including secure storage, limited access and staff training

9. Your Rights Under NDPA 2023

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent (where processing is consent-based)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC)

To exercise these rights, contact us at the details below

10. Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law (e.g., student records retained for statutory periods after leaving school)

11. Cookies & Website Tracking

This site uses minimal essential cookies. We do not use advanced tracking or advertising cookies. You can manage preferences via your browser settings

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our services/website after changes constitutes acceptance
